Customer Charter
#All About You
We are committed to providing you, with banking that is simple to understand and delivered in a responsible manner in accordance with the highest standards of integrity.
We are here to serve you and in order to do that, we have created a Customer Charter – our promise to you.
To communicate with you in a way that is simple, personable, fair, and engaging.
We are ready to listen, learn, reflect and improve taking into account your needs - not just today but throughout your journey with us.
We are serious about your future – so let us take you there.
