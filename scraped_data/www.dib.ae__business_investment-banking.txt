Investment Banking
OUR SCOPE
As a leading player in the Islamic finance market, Dubai Islamic Bank brings unmatched experience to a host of patrons ranging from corporate, institutional and sovereign / quasi-sovereign clients, assisting with every aspect of their finance requirements. Dedicated professionals provide innovative Sharia compliant capital raising and financing solutions in line with evolving customer requirements and market conditions.
OUR PRODUCTS
Our diversified product suite includes a wide range with particular focus on Sukuk Structuring & Execution, Syndicated Facilities, Project Finance & Club Facilities that are structured and documented in accordance with principles of Sharia. DIB has been a significant player in the Debt Capital and Bank Syndication market closely working with local and international banks as Joint MLA and originator.
FEATURES
The main credit related products on offer include:
'Letter of Guarantee' required in accordance with contract terms
'Letters of Credit' with deferred payment facility through Murabaha payment structures
Mudarabaha structure to finance a projects’ funding requirements and general working capital needs
Ijarah (Lease purchase) for financing capital goods
