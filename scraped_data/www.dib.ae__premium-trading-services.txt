Premium Trading Services
Access comprehensive brokerage services to trade in financial markets via DIB’s strategic partner, Al Ramz Capital LLC.
Strategic Alliance
Dubai Islamic Bank and Al Ramz Corporation announced a strategic alliance on 17 November, 2022, wherein the bank would provide brokerage and related services in financial markets to its clients through Al Ramz Capital, a subsidiary of the Al Ramz Corporation.
How to open an account
To begin the online onboarding process,
click here
, fill your details and a representative from Al Ramz will contact you at the earliest.
Al Ramz
Al Ramz has been a key player in the UAE financial sector since 1998, with a proven track record of market leadership, a strong financial base, strict compliance with laws and regulations, as well as a constant drive for growth and expansion.
By opening an account with Al Ramz, you will enjoy the following services:
Islamic trading mode that will allow you to trade only in Sharia-compliant shares
10-minute online onboarding process
Access to UAE markets, ADX, DFM and Nasdaq Dubai
Access to Saudi markets from the same platform
Access to Oman, Bahrain and Astana stock markets from the same platform through ADX Tabadul Hub
Insightful research report and daily technical analysis reports
An online trading platform
Same day fund transfer (inwards and outwards) using your DIB bank account
For further information about Al Ramz, visit their website
